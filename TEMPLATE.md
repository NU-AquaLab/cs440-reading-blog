# Post Template

`YYYY-MM-DD-short-title.md`

---

## Key Idea

What problem is the paper solving and what is the core contribution? (2–3 sentences)

## Critique

What is convincing, what is not, what is missing? (2–3 sentences)

## Connections

How does this relate to earlier readings? Link to your previous posts where
relevant — for example:

> This rethinks the same fairness assumptions I discussed in
> [my Week 4 post on congestion signals](/2026/10/14/no-signal-to-rule-them-all/).

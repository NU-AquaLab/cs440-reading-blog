# Post Template

Copy this template to `_posts/` with the filename format:
`YYYY-MM-DD-short-title.md`

---

```markdown
---
layout: post
title: "Short Descriptive Title"
date: YYYY-MM-DD
paper_authors: "Author1, Author2, Author3"
paper_venue: "Conference Year"
paper_url: "https://..."
week: N
tags: [topic1, topic2]
---

## Problem

What problem is this paper solving? What was the state of the world before it?

## Key Idea

What is the main contribution? What is the core technical insight?

## Evaluation

How did the authors evaluate their work? Is the evaluation sound?

## Critique

What do you find convincing or unconvincing? What is missing?

## Connections

How does this paper relate to others we have read? Link to your earlier posts
where relevant — for example:

> This rethinks the same fairness assumptions I discussed in
> [my Week 4 post on congestion signals](/2026/10/14/no-signal-to-rule-them-all/).

## Takeaway

One or two sentences: what will you remember from this paper in a year?
```
